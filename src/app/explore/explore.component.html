Anime Genre & Sub-Genre's

Sports

Sports anime are shows that cover characters engaging in athletic competition.

Focuses on character and team development, and highlights action-packed moments.

Slice of Life

Stories depicted in real life.

Yōkai

Yōkai are creature in old Japanese stories, such as oni, kappa, or tengu.

The most famous yōkai are Tengu, Oni (Japanese ogre), and Kyubi no Kitsune (a fox with nine tails).

Seinen

For young men over 18 years old.

Action, comedy, drama and even hentai can all be called seinen.

Shōjo

Word meaning "Girl".

Most shōjo characters are female.

Stories mainly focus on love, friendship, and romance.

Yuri

Yuri or shojo-ai are Anime about girls who are in love with other girls.

Also called "S-class" and "Girls Love".

Shotacon

Shota means little boy. Shotacon is someone who likes little boys.

Is a Japanese slang of the phrase Shōtarō complex.

It describes an attraction to young boys around 13 years old or younger.

Ecchi

Means pervert in Japanese.

Ecchi comes from the sound of H in the word hentai.

Full of sexually provocative scenes.

Shounen

Usually a combination of action and adventure.

Relatively simple plots and focus on action.

Typically adapted from serial manga series.

Mecha

Mecha stands for mechanical (as in Gigantic Robots).

Mecha's will always have a pilot.

Isekai

Isekai translates to "another world".

Protagonist somehow gets transported to a different world and gets amazing power.

Harem

Male character surrounded by multiple female characters.

More than two female characters go head-over-heels for a single male character.

Josei

For young women over 18 years old.

More mature anime, usually more like real life for women.

Yaoi

Yaoi or shounen-ai are Anime about boys who are in love with other boys.

Also called "boys Love". Most yaoi fans are female.

Almost always has an emotionless expression.

Lolicon

Is a slang word for the phrase "Lolita complex".

It describes an attraction to young girls around 13 years old or younger.

Can be anywhere from an adult man too a young girl who desires other young girls.
